# 🍽️ AURUM – Fine Dining Experience

**AURUM** is more than a restaurant website — it’s a digital journey into the heart of haute cuisine. Crafted for Michelin-style experiences, it merges elegance, emotion, and modern web interactivity to immerse your guests even before they walk in.  

---

## 🌟 Why AURUM?

In the world of fine dining, first impressions are everything. This website is designed to:

- **Engage the senses** — visuals, typography, and motion mimic the feeling of being in a luxury restaurant.
- **Build trust instantly** — showcase the chef, awards, reviews, and events elegantly.
- **Convert curiosity into reservation** — interactive forms, loyalty programs, and newsletter subscription guide users naturally.
- **Deliver a premium experience** — smooth animations, custom cursors, parallax effects, and subtle micro-interactions elevate user experience.

## 🎯 Key Features

### **1. Hero & Visual Identity**
- Full-screen hero with subtle parallax scroll.
- Emotionally resonant typography and color palette (`Gold`, `Dark`, `Muted`) to evoke luxury.

### **2. Menu & Offerings**
- Interactive menu tabs (Cocktails, Teas, Wines, Signature Dishes).
- Tag-based filters (Vegan, Non-Alcoholic, Signature) for easy browsing.
- Dynamic pricing layout, ready for future e-commerce integration.

### **3. Reservation System**
- Smart form with date, time, guests, and special requests.
- Real-time validation and min-date restriction.
- Engaging success animations to reward user action.

### **4. Reviews & Social Proof**
- Slider with automated and manual navigation.
- Stars, reviewer info, and locations for credibility.
- Emotionally framed content: storytelling, not just data.

### **5. Chef & Experience**
- Highlight Executive Chef, awards, and philosophy.
- Emotional storytelling: connects guest to brand persona.
- CTA: “Dine with the Chef” to trigger reservation engagement.

### **6. Events & Loyalty**
- Event cards with dates, description, and pricing.
- Tiered membership system for exclusivity and repeat engagement.
- CTA buttons for each tier: “Join Free”, “Become Gold”, “Inquire”.

### **7. Newsletter**
- Elegant subscription form with validation.
- Reward-based incentive: discounts and complimentary offerings.
- Reinforces long-term engagement and brand loyalty.

### **8. Cart & Ordering (Future Ready)**
- Sidebar cart with add/remove items.
- Visual feedback for user interactions.
- Prepared for advanced e-commerce integration.

### **9. Interactive Enhancements**
- Custom cursor with dynamic hover effects.
- Intersection Observer for smooth reveal animations.
- Subtle parallax for hero and hero-related images.
- Responsive navbar and mobile menu.

## 💻 Technologies Used

- HTML5 (semantic, structured for luxury UX)
- CSS3 (Grid, Flexbox, variables, transitions, hover effects)
- JavaScript ES6 (interactive features: cart, reviews, reservation, tabs, cursor)
- Intersection Observer API (scroll reveals)
- Optional: ready for **WordPress headless or hybrid integration**
